The light was barely ahead of me when I reached the lane.  I was just barely idling along at this point, so as not to catch up with it!  While it continued up the hill I turned onto the lane and stomped on the accelerator.  I looked in my rearview mirror and saw it behind me.  I started honking my horn and as soon as I was in my parent's drivway.  The lights in my parent's house came on and they came out onto the porch.  I stopped the car and ran as fast as I could into the house.  It took my parents a long while to calm me down. My parents ran into the room, turnng on lights as they made their way to the living room.  The light seemed to dissapate and disappear.
