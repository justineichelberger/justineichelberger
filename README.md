Upon backing out of the driveway friend #4, sitting in the back passenger side said "What in the heck is that?"  Nobody looked because she has a drug problem and we never take her seriously. I came to a stop sign within a mile, rolled through it and the object was moving closer to us from the direction of Friend #1 house. Most of it follows a bayou for the first 5 miles or so.  Then it is open pastures and farm land for 5 or 6 miles to a city of 20,000. I live on the opposite side of town so we drove through town on a four lane highway until we stopped at a gas station 3 miles from my home.  Then we proceeded to my house through farm land.  Through the entire route the object stayed to our left as we headed north and then west.  It was hard to see it clear when we first left Friend #1's house due to the trees but we could see it. It was the first time any of us saw a spark of the normal Friend #1 in the past few days. No one outside of my car seemed to see it.  We were all looking up and while on the 4-lane I watched other cars and it was as if they didn't see us either but that's not unusual these days. I never noticed that it would cause a blind spot when you try to see past the fluorescent lights at dusk.    While we were in the store I remembered I had a dang camera in the car!.  After 5 minutes we started back out and it was waiting on us over a field still to the left, passed a bridge.   Now it was dark. It now had red, white and blue lights (we triple checked that that night). After listening to coast to coast, I have realized that may not have been the smartest move.
