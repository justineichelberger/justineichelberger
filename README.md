After observing the light for several minutes, I called my brother who lives approximately 10 miles away and asked him if he was able to see the light as well. My brother was able to see the light for approximately another 1/2 hour because his home is not surrounded by trees, as mine is. My brother also thought that he saw another similar light source due west at a higher altitude.  I looked there as well to confirm his sighting of a second similar light source, and saw it as well, but it was not nearly as bright, and to be honest, while it looked similar, I could not make it out well enough to say with 100% certainly that it was also pulsing blue and red. I understand that early this morning there was a docking, so perhaps what we were watching was the docking lights of the space station. Also, unless they were testing the docking lights the night before, docking lights would not explain why my daughter's friend saw the same  lights the night before. I never hear any mention of the reported lights on the news, I can only assume they thought it was a hoax. However I can say with 100% certainty, that the lights were indeed pulsing red, blue and white. PD))</sub></blockquote><sub><sub><i>source: [http://www.nuforc.org/webreports/reports/043/S43523.html](http://www.nuforc.org/webreports/reports/043/S43523.html)</i></sub></sub></sub><br>
