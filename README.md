But the whole story needed to be told, as my husband has NEVER been the same.  It shook him to his very core and would refuse to talk about it as it brought back the intense fear.After a while the fireball on top of the mountain slowly began to fade, but remained glowing for about two hours.  I get angry at myself for not taking the truck up there to see for myself, but it's very rugged terrain to get up there even w/ a dirt road or a fire road on which to travel.
