After a short walk south my friend suddenly stopped and was peering south south-east slightly out towards the ocean before I could ask him why he stopped  a bright orange glow took hold of my attention in the direction he was staring. Both of our eyes were affixed to this point where this glow took up a good chunk of the sky it looked as if a bomb had gone off. Although surprised we continued to watch the object astounded that we were the only two watching this great event. Throughout my entire life I've never witnessed Helicopters, planes, balloons, shuttles, or any other type  of aircraft behave the way this object did.
