It became increasingly difficult to focus on the portion of the sky that the UFO was located.  We had to avert our eyes many times due to the power emitted.  Then, all of a sudden, it was gone.  There wasn't a trace.  The sky also resumed its normal brilliance though to our eyes everything took on a glummer hue.This may not be the most fantastic form of a tale but it really did happen.  After that, the three of us shared a couple of stories of previous encounters, or at the very least, we thought them to be encounters.  To this day, we talk about the strangeness of the event.
