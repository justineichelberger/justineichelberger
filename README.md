of it. he didnt see it. when it was directly above us it couldnt have been more than three hundred feet, but the only way to detect it was by the blocking out of the stars. once i focused all the details were evident. the first thing i thought was that this thing was ancient. it looked like a peice of the grand canyon. the second thing i thought was this thing is heading towards toronto. the
